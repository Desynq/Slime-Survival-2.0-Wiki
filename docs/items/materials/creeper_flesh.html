Creeper Flesh

You may have already noticed that creepers do not drop gunpowder.

Creepers instead now drop creeper flesh (0-1 without looting, 0-2 with looting).

Creeper flesh is mostly useful for explosive production but can also be eaten with explosive
side effects, with any subsequent damage from eating said flesh being neglegible for creepers.

It should, however, be noted that creepers may become diseased when consuming creeper flesh.
